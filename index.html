1. List of 114 Surahs and Their Names

  • 1. Al-Fatiha - Revealed in Makkah
  • 2. Al-Baqarah - Revealed in Madina

2. List of 50 Miracles of the Qur'an

  • 1. The miracle of preservation – The Qur'an remains unchanged since its revelation.
  • 2. The scientific insights on embryology – Mentioned in Surah Al-Mu’minun.
  • 3. The splitting of the moon – As mentioned in Surah Al-Qamar.

3. List of 50 Blessings of the Qur'an

  • 1. Recitation of Surah Al-Fatiha brings healing and mercy.
  • 2. Surah Al-Baqarah offers protection from Shaytan.
  • 3. Surah Yasin is known as the heart of the Qur'an, bringing many rewards.

4. List of 50 Shifa Verses from the Qur'an

  • 1. Surah Al-Fatiha (1:1-7) - Recite over the sick with intention for healing.
  • 2. Surah Al-Baqarah (2:255) - Ayat al-Kursi, recite for protection and healing.
  • 3. Surah Al-Isra (17:82) - "And We send down of the Qur'an that which is healing and mercy..."

5. Other Important Parts of the Qur'an

  • 1. The Last Two Verses of Surah Al-Baqarah - For divine protection.
  • 2. Surah Al-Kahf - Recite every Friday for protection from the Dajjal.
  • 3. Surah Al-Mulk - Recite before sleep to be saved from the punishment of the grave.
